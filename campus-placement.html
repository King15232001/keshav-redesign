Placement Support

Career Preparation Areas

Students should prepare for career opportunities through academic knowledge, communication skills, confidence and practical awareness.

01

Career Guidance

Students are guided to understand suitable career paths based on their programme and interest.

02

Resume Preparation

Students should prepare a clear resume with academic details, skills and achievements.

03

Interview Readiness

Interview preparation helps students build confidence and present themselves professionally.

04

Communication Skills

Students should improve speaking, writing and professional communication skills.

05

Skill Development

Skill development activities improve employability, confidence and practical readiness.

06

Academic Strength

Strong subject knowledge and academic performance support future opportunities.

07

Professional Values

Discipline, responsibility, punctuality and ethical conduct are important for career growth.

08

Higher Education

Students may also prepare for higher studies, competitive exams and professional courses.

Student Preparation Guidance

Career preparation should begin during the academic session. Students should use classroom learning, library resources, seminars, workshops, NSS, skill development activities and faculty guidance to prepare for future opportunities.

A
Build Academic Foundation

Focus on syllabus, regular classes, subject clarity and examination performance.

B
Prepare Professional Profile

Maintain a simple resume with academic details, skills, activities and achievements.

C
Improve Communication

Practice speaking, writing, group discussion, basic computer use and interview communication.

D
Participate in Activities

Attend seminars, workshops, skill development sessions and campus activities for exposure.
