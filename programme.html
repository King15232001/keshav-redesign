Academic Programmes

UG, PG and Integrated Programmes

Keshav Mahavidyalaya offers a structured academic environment through Education, Social Science and Science departments. The programmes are designed to support students in higher education, subject knowledge, professional preparation and overall development.

Students should carefully review programme details, syllabus, admission process, academic calendar and department information before applying for admission.

Department-Wise Programmes

Programme Details

The following programme structure is arranged department-wise so that students can easily understand available courses.

01

Department of Education

Teacher education and integrated education programmes.

B.Sc.-B.Ed.

Integrated teacher education programme for students with science background.

B.A.-B.Ed.

Integrated teacher education programme for students with arts/social science background.

B.Ed.

Teacher education programme focused on pedagogy, professional training and classroom preparation.

02

Department of Social Science

Undergraduate and postgraduate programmes in humanities and social science subjects.

B.A.

Hindi Litt., Sanskrit, Geography, Pol. Science, Home Science and Sociology with Compulsory Subjects.

M.A. HindiLitt.

Postgraduate programme in Hindi Literature.

M.A. Geography

Postgraduate programme in Geography.

M.A. Sociology

Postgraduate programme in Sociology.

M.A. Pol. Science

Postgraduate programme in Political Science.

03

Department of Science

Science programmes with academic and practical learning support.

B.Sc.

Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Zoology, Botany with Compulsory Subjects.

M.Sc. Chemistry

Postgraduate programme in Chemistry.

M.Sc. Zoology

Postgraduate programme in Zoology.
