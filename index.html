About Organization

Keshav Mahavidyalaya

KeshavMahavidyalaya, Atru, is one of the prestigious institutions of higher education in Rajasthan. The college works with the objective of providing value-added quality education to students, with special emphasis on accessible and meaningful learning.

The institution focuses on academic development, ethical values, leadership qualities, teamwork, critical thinking and a research-oriented learning culture. The website has been structured to provide students, parents, faculty members and visitors with direct access to admission, academics, NAAC, IQAC, approvals, facilities and contact information.
