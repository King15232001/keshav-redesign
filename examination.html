Examination Preparation Guidance

Students should prepare for examinations with a proper study plan. Preparation should be based on syllabus, classroom teaching, academic calendar and faculty guidance.

A
Prepare Unit-Wise

Divide the syllabus into units and prepare each unit with notes, questions and revision points.

B
Revise Regularly

Do not wait until the final exam period. Revise topics weekly to improve retention and confidence.

C
Use Library and Resources

Use library books, classroom notes, faculty guidance and available academic resources for better preparation.

D
Follow Exam Discipline

Reach the examination centre on time, carry required documents and follow all examination rules.
